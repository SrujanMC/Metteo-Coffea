Heart of Bengaluru

Steps away from Blossom Book House, Matteo Coffea sits in the city's most iconic street. A premium café experience with rich aroma, cozy ambiance, and urban energy.

Church Street is the soul of Bengaluru's walking culture, and we provide the perfect sanctuary to pause. Whether you are browsing books at Blossoms or enjoying a stroll near MG Road, our doors are open for that perfect cup of artisanal coffee.

Our space is designed to blend the high-energy vibe of the street with a sophisticated, calm interior. It's where deep conversations happen over freshly baked croissants and signature brews.
